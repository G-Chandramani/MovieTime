About the Movie

In Crystal Peaks forest, bear cubs Briar and Bramble lose their mother, Barbara, in a fire. Determined to find her, they meet Charlotte, whose amber stone gives them hope. Amidst challenges, they discover a Scrap Rebel member who resembles their lost mother. This revelation drives them to confront the truth, leading to victory and uncovering life-changing secrets.
